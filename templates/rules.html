Official Rules

King vs Pawns

A minimalist chess variant testing the King's agility against a wall of advancing Pawns. There are no captures - only movement and positioning.

Initial Setup

King: Starts on d1

Pawns: Start on a8, c8, e8, g8

The King moves first.

Movement

The King

Moves exactly one square diagonally in any direction. It cannot jump or move orthogonally.

The Pawns

Move exactly one square diagonally forward (downward). They cannot move sideways or backward.

Winning Conditions
King Wins: Reaches any square on the 8th rank (Row 7).
Pawns Win: Traps the King so it has no legal moves remaining.
